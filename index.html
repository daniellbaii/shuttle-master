Meet Your Coach

Eveshgaran Vasigaran is a former Malaysian national badminton player with over 15 years of professional experience. He has competed internationally, including participation in tournaments such as the Bangka Belitung Indonesia Masters.

Currently, Evesh serves as the Senior Pro (Captain) at PBX Badminton Team in Western Australia, where he holds the top ranking in men's singles.

Evesh is passionate about coaching and is dedicated to helping players of all levels improve their skills and achieve their badminton goals.
